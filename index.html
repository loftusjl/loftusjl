About Me

I am a full-Stack developer with an extensive background in database development. A fast learner and a highly focused, organized, professional with secret clearance and 16 years federal employment in both aircraft rework and hospital equipment management industries.

I am constantly learning and have taken college courses on database design, SQL, Web Development, Java and A+ certification. Currently, I am working to further develop my technical skills by attending the UC San Diego Extension Code bootcamp for full-stack development.

I have always been drawn to software and web development which led me to build and currently maintain the Equipment Office Manager (EOM) which has been utilized by the Navy Medicine South West Region since 2011. EOM holds 2,980 Command purchase records along with scans of the original purchase request for audit readiness and totals $231M to-date. In addition, EOM has been used as the equipment receiving system and currently holds records for 29,530 pieces of equipment valued at $186.7M. For developing the EOM, I was awarded Equipment Manager of the year by the Navy Bureau of Medicine and Surgery.

Through my developing the EOM I found great enjoyment working directly with end-users to develop a usable system that would provide increased reliability and efficiency in their day to day work lives.
